Learn To Play Click Battle!

Easy to play, impossible to win!!

To start, simply press 'Begin Game'

Immediately after beginning the game, you'll click the 'Click Fast' button as fast as you can, racing the computer.

Whoever reaches 25 first, wins!

You also start off with 10 bombs, press the 'F' key to use a bomb. Bombs reset the computer's score to 0. Use them wisely!
